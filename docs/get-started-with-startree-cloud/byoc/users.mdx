# Users

Users are cloud administrators with privileges to administer their own public cloud infrastructure (AWS or GCP) and want to create, list, view, and delete StarTree environments.

To register an account, navigate to the [StarTree Cloud](https://startree.cloud/) home page.
You have two options for creating an account:

* Google - Use your Google login credentials
* Email Address - Create a username and password.

### Google Account

1. Click **Continue with Google**
2. Enter one of the following identifiers for your Google account:
    * Your Gmail or Google Apps email address
    * The phone number associated with your Google account
3. Click **Next**
4. Enter the password for your Google account
5. Click **Next**
6. Complete the 2-Step Verification step (if enabled)

You should now be logged into your StarTree Cloud account.

### Email Address

1. Click **Sign up**
2. Enter your email address and a password that you'd like to use for this account
<Callout type="info">
Your password must contain:

* At least 8 characters
* At least 3 of the following:
    * Lower case letters (a-z)
    * Upper case letters (A-Z)
    * Numbers (0-9)
    * Special characters (ex. !@#$%^&*)
</Callout>

3. Click **Continue**
4. Click **Accept** to authorize StarTree Cloud.

You should now be logged into your StarTree Cloud account.
